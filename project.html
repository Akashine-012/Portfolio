Project 1

Topic: 'Renewable Source Vehicle'

This project motive is to create a global and eco-friendly environment. Renewable source vehicles are powered by energy derived from renewable resources such as electricity, hydrogen, solar energy, and biofuels. These vehicles aim to reduce reliance on fossil fuels, lower greenhouse gas emissions, and promote sustainable transportation.

Project 2

Topic: 'Hostel Management System'

This project helps in various ways. A Hostel Management System is a software solution designed to streamline and automate the administrative tasks involved in managing a hostel or dormitory. This system ensures efficient handling of various activities such as room allocation, resident records, fee collection, maintenance requests, and more.
